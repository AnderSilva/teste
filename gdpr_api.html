Data Deletion Instructions

To request the deletion of your personal data from our application, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to me.remove@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion".
  2. In the body of the email, include your full name and the user ID associated with your account.
  3. Briefly describe which data you would like to have deleted.

Once we receive your request, we will process the data deletion within 30 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is completed.

If you have any questions, please contact us at the same email address.
